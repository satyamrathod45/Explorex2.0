EARTH

Earth is a rocky planet with a solid surface and a thick atmosphere. The planet is 70% water and 30% land. The water includes oceans, lakes, rivers, streams, rain, snow, and ice. The atmosphere is made up of 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and 1% other gases

MOON

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and the fifth-largest moon in the Solar System. It is about 1/4th the size of Earth, with a diameter of 3,474 km (2,159 miles). The Moon is approximately 384,400 km (238,855 miles) away from Earth.
